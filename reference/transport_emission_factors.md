# Transport Emission Factors Dataset

This dataset contains emission factors for different modes of transport,
categorized by country.

## Usage

``` r
data(transport_emission_factors)
```

## Format

A data frame with columns:

- Country:

  Character, name of the country or region

- Diesel_Vehicle:

  Numeric, carbon emissions from diesel vehicles

- Electric_Vehicle:

  Numeric, carbon emissions from electric vehicles

- Flights_Long:

  Numeric, emissions from long-haul flights

- Flights_Short:

  Numeric, emissions from short-haul flights

- Gasoline_Vehicle:

  Numeric, emissions from gasoline vehicles

- Hybrid_Vehicle:

  Numeric, emissions from hybrid vehicles

- Long_Distance_Train:

  Numeric, emissions from train travel

- Natural_Gas_Vehicle:

  Numeric, emissions from natural gas vehicles

- Public_Transport:

  Numeric, emissions from public transport
